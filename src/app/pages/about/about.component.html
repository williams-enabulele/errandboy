About us

Errandboyservices is a specialized online platform created to provide on-demand pickup and delivery services in the US. We offer an exclusive range of leading pickup and drop-off services for individuals who may be too busy to collect purchases or assist those with disabilities in receiving their groceries, prescriptions, and more. Our pickup and delivery team is also specialized in helping local businesss in the New York Area. We presently serve Manhattan, the Bronx and Westchester, New York.

What We Do

We offer the following service solutions

  • Prescription pickup and delivery

  • Laundry or dry-cleaning pickup and drop-off

  • Bakery pickup and delivery

  • Mail or package pickup and delivery

  • Store pickup or purchase and delivery

  • Catering pickup and delivery


We pickup up from pharmacies and general stores of your choice
Our drivers are well trained and licensed to provide the most professional service delivery to you as our truly valued customers. We maintain your confidentiality every step of the way and ensure that all customer information is only used for exceptional service delivery.
We offer 7 days of pickup and delivery services from 5am to 9pm. Our online booking service is available 24 hours, 7 days a week.
